About Boycie Bear

Boycie was born September 29th 2017 in Shrewsbury. He was one of three Chocolate Labradors born in a litter of seven and was the biggest puppy of all his brothers and sisters.

He was named after one our favourite Only Fools and Horses characters Mr Boyce(Boycie).
